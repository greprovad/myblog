Reading Culture On Social Media

May 29, 2018    Reading Culture Social Media

One of the most important heritages the world has to carefully conserve is reading culture. Social media (SM) constitute a threat for the future since they augment distraction and absorb concentration. Some memes, posts and fake news bring confusion and affect the stability of brain development. One may be interested in a blog title, but while opening this latter, the content diametrically narrates the opposite of what the title should address. Therefore, this results in a waste of time and installs the reluctance to open other attractive blog posts by the title that might appear on SM timeline.

SM companies should not only care about contents that convey contempt, racism, terrorist attacks but also irrelevant contents that can easily cross users’ subconscious and produce irreversible long-term consequences in their mind.
